Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new course?

You can create a new course by navigating to the Dashboard and clicking the "Create Class" button. Fill out the course detils such as Subject Code, Term, and click submit.

How do I update my profile picture?

Navigate to the Profile tab in the sidebar, click the "Edit Profile" button, and select "Upload New Picture" in the photo section.

Can I delegate grading to an assistant?

Yes, from a specific course's detail page, you can manage the course roster and add users with the "Grading Assistant" role. They will be able to see and grade submissions.
